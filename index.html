Tell good stories. Show your impact.

We’ve worked with a lot of charities. They have amazing stories to tell about the people they work with. But they don’t always have the resources to show the world the difference they're making.

Writegood is a storytelling app that makes the writing process simple and rewarding. We’re currently working on a first prototype and have lots of ideas, including:

  • - Plain English recommendations
  • - Focussed writing modes
  • - Guided templates to help you create engaging content
  • - Exporting features.
